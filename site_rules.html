Site Rules

  1. Respectful Conduct:

    Users must maintain a respectful and civil demeanor towards each other. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks will be tolerated. This includes respecting diverse opinions and viewpoints.

  2. Original Content:

    When posting memes, users should ensure that they are either original creations or properly credited if sourced from elsewhere. Plagiarism or claiming someone else's work as your own is prohibited.

  3. No Spoilers:

    To respect the experience of others, avoid posting spoilers without proper warnings. Use spoiler tags or clearly label content that may reveal significant storylines, plot twists, or game outcomes.

  4. Appropriate Content:

    Memes should be suitable for all audiences. Avoid posting explicit, offensive, or NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content. This includes images, text, or any material that may be considered inappropriate.

  5. Stay On Topic:

    Content shared on the site should primarily relate to Baldur's Gate 3. While some off-topic discussion might be acceptable, the focus should generally remain on the game and its related memes.

  6. No Spam or Self-Promotion:

    Refrain from excessive self-promotion or spamming of links, unrelated content, or advertisements. This includes repeatedly posting the same content or links.

  7. Respect Copyright:

    Users must adhere to copyright laws when sharing memes. Avoid using copyrighted material without proper permissions, and respect the intellectual property rights of others.

  8. Moderator Discretion:

    Site moderators reserve the right to remove, edit, or take action against any content or user violating these rules. Their decisions should be respected and followed.

  9. Feedback and Reporting:

    Users are encouraged to provide feedback and report any content that violates these rules. Reporting inappropriate content helps maintain a healthy and enjoyable community environment.

  10. Have Fun and Contribute Positively:

    Ultimately, the aim of the community is to have fun, share laughter, and contribute positively to the Baldur's Gate 3 fandom through creative and humorous memes while respecting others within the community.
